Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC)

Our DCC solution allows international cardholders to pay in their home currency directly at the Point of Sale. It enhances the customer experience by providing full transparency on exchange rates, while offering merchants and acquirers an additional revenue stream.

Fleet Card System

A comprehensive fleet card management system tailored for gas stations and corporate fleets. It allows deep integration with terminal points to manage spending limits, track driver activity, and optimize corporate fuel expenditures securely.

Installment Payment Plan (IPP)

Flexible installment payment solutions that allow merchants to offer "buy now, pay later" features directly at the checkout. Our engine effortlessly handles complex payment splitting and bank integration to ensure seamless approvals.

Transaction Gateway & Switching

A robust and secure payment gateway for routing and switching transactions across multiple legacy and modern systems. We reformat messages in real-time, bridging the gap between old infrastructures and new digital wallets seamlessly.

Recurring Payment System

Automate subscription billing and recurring payments securely with our flexible processing engine. Perfect for utility companies, insurance, fitness clubs, and SaaS providers who need reliable scheduled payment execution.

Alipay & WeChat Acquiring System

Seamlessly integrate with China's top digital wallets to accept payments from Chinese tourists and cross-border shoppers. Our acquiring system processes QR code payments rapidly and safely.
